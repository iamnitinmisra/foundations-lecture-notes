Why is QA important?
	Saves money
	increases confidence
	smoother releases

What is a test plan?
	a collection of test cases
	
what is a test case?
	a step by step process in order to test a specific aspect of your product
	
what is a bug report?
	a report that lists all of the failures that were found in each test case
	
what should be included in a bug report?
	description of the issue, steps to reproduce the issue, what the bug was, including platform that was tested on, date, product version that was tested
	
how do you determine the risk of a specific bug?
	cost * liklihood to occur = risk
	
why is documentation important?
	it helps us keep track of issues
